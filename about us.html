SKELEM is a local a local registered clothing brand owned by a young man Bongani Bobo Tevez, aims to promote South African fashion and culture. Skelem's mission is to build a retail space whereby South African brands lead, while also acknowledging South African excellence and independence. The brand envisions a united, economic intergeneration across South Africa. The brand is mostly for people who commonly have a unique fashion sense. By highlighting the brands's story and products, we ca effectively promote South African culture and unique sense of fashion to a wider audience.
